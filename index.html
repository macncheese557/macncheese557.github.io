All about Dogs!

Basic info

The dog is a very faithful, pet animal. It is a four-footed pet animal. It has two eyes, two ears, sharp teeth and a short tail. It has four thin legs. They help them to run very fast. It eats many things like meat, fish, milk, vegetables, rice, etc.
It is a very intelligent animal. It generally watches its owner's house. They always remain obedient and faithful to their owner. They sometimes save the lives of their owners. Thieves, notorious criminals are able to be arrested with the help of a dog. They are the most useful friends of humankind. There is no other animal like it.
